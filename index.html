Creative Coding Mechanics

Hand Tracking: The application leverages Google's MediaPipe model via WebGL and CPU pipelines to map 21 landmark coordinate positions in real-time.

Signed Distance Fields (SDFs): We feed fingertips and scale vectors directly as arrays of dynamic uniforms into a high-performance GLSL fragment shader, creating organic, overlapping "bulges" as your hands near the screen.

Wave Physics: Moving your hand rapidly triggers physical wavefront calculations on a custom wave-packet algorithm inside the shader, creating radiating liquid ripples.

Optical Properties: Features realistic rendering techniques including chromatic aberration (lens light dispersion), Fresnel reflection (increased gloss on shallow angles), and thin-film interference to synthesize soap-bubble iridescence.
